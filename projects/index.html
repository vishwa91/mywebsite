Latest project

Working on USBtiny based servo motor controller for simple 2D plotter.

Accessible Reading Material

The project is under the guidance of Dr. Anil Prabhakar and Dr. Sujatha Srinivasan. The aim is to make a low cost plotter that will make simple children's picture books tactilable. The project is called Tactograph, after Tactiling Pantograph. The plotter is a 5 links robot, with two stepper motors as actuators. The device is based on PIC18F4550 USB device. A provisional patent has been filed(Patent Application No. 4091/CHE/ 2011“Tactograph”Date of Entry 28th November 2011 in the names ofIndian Institute of Technology- Madras and Chetana Charitable Trust) and a patent is under process.

The project has been selected for IEEE EPICS competition and IEEE AIYEHUM competition. The project was a part of Innovative Students Project(IC&SR, IITMadras).
